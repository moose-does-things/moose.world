Why a html website?

I used to attend a private school that only taught Cambridge across all year levels. I got moved up in Year 10 and was doing IGCSE courses, one of which was a requirement was IT. It was these old ass textbooks that had half confusing excel spreadsheet formulas that didn't work and half html. This meant that I was not only taught hella weird and use html and css in the same page on notepad++, but when it came time to take the mid-year test I almost failed the excel work and nailed the html. Whether that actually was because of my natural knack for it or because we'd been taught html since year 7 I'm not sure. Either way I'm an idiot who loves crappy retro tech, and html websites just do it for me. So here we are. Take a look around and if Tristan is reading this: STAY TF OUT OF MY CODE.
